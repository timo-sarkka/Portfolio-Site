About me.

I first became interested in the world of data and AI back in 2016. I realized then, that there was already a multitude of learning resources on the web that anyone could use freely, at any time. I looked around and even started studying some topics, but I was working full time as a project manager at the time. My days were long, and there was a lot of travelling involved. I just couldn't find the time to also squeeze in studying. I decided to put my studies on hold and concentrate on my career in another industry.

Fast forward to year 2019, when I ran across several new data and AI accomplishments, such as StyleGAN, AlphaGo's performance and recognizing skin cancer. It was remarkable how far AI and machine learning had already come in just a few years.

I knew then that I had to get involved and start understanding these topics which probably will shape our future for the years to come. I started from the basics and signed up at self-paced learning platforms such as Dataquest and Team Treehouse. After a few months of studying I had a good basic understanding of python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, SQL and statistics. I also complemented my studies with web technologies, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP.

So, here I am! Still in the start of my journey but learning more every day.
