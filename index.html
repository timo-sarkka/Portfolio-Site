Hi, I'm Timo.

I have managed industrial projects of different sizes for the better part of the last 7 years. My roots are in the maritime industry. I have worked in design offices, on factory floors and at shipyards.

Lately, I've taken a turn to learn about IT and data. I'm especially interested in IT project management, data analytics and AI/ML. Hopefully I can learn and leverage these topics in my future roles.

Some principles I try to live by: hard (but smart) work pays off; simplify until you can't; listen to your gut; delayed over instant gratification; and underpromise - overdeliver.

When not on the job, I enjoy various kinds of sports (functional training, weightlifting, running), spend time with my dog or just listen to good music (electronic, 80s or both combined!).
